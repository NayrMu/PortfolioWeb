About Me

Born and raised in Independence, Missouri, I embarked on my journey into the world of software development. I started college for Computer Science at Missouri State University in 2019. However, after struggling with my schooling during the quarentine in 2020, I decided to step way from academia in 2021. For two enriching years, I delved into full-time work, learning more about the world and myself. Now, I'm back in academia at Western Governor's University, pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, set to graduate in June 2025.

My fascination, for software crosses multiple disciplines, from crafting elegant websites to breathing life into applications and delving into the intricate world of embedded systems! Some of my favorite projects include a C# CLI tool, a rocket fuel calculation Python program, and the website you're exploring now. This is just the start of my journey, with a universe of ideas yet to explore. Join me as I continue pushing the boundaries of software, one line of code at a time.
